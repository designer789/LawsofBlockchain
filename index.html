How to Use

Here's a simple guide to managing your Laws of Blockchain investment account via Telegram:

01

Start the Bot

Search for @LawsOfBlockchainBot on Telegram and click "Start" to activate it.

02

Check Your Balance

Type: /balance

The bot replies with your invested amount, yields, and growth percentage.

03

Review Performance

Type: /performance

Get a summary of your investment gains over a specific time period.

04

Get Market Insights

Type: /insights

Receive real-time trends and risk levels for key assets.

05

Make a Deposit

Type: /deposit

The bot provides a wallet address to send your SOL for investment.

06

Request a Withdrawal

Type: /withdraw [amount]

Specify the SOL amount you want to withdraw; the bot will process it.

07

Need Help?

Type: /help

See a list of all commands and their functions.

That's it! Use these commands to manage your account directly from Telegram.
